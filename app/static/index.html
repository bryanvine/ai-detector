Method

Text is scored token-by-token under open-weight language models (qwen3-30b-tq, gpt-oss-120b) — perplexity, rank and rhythm statistics — plus stylometry and an LLM judge. Images are checked for generator provenance (C2PA, SD/ComfyUI chunks), frequency-domain and sensor-noise fingerprints, and a learned classifier. Video adds frame-by-frame classification and temporal noise coherence. Signals fuse into one calibrated probability. No detector is perfect; treat scores near 50% as genuinely uncertain.
